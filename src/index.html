Hacker Flairgrounds

"Flaming badge builder or just badge curious, Hacker Flairgrounds is the ultimate gathering of hackers and blinking LEDs in Vegas."

The destination for badge collectors, designers, and hardware hackers to celebrate the flashier side of DEF CON. It is a melding of the 1337 and the un1eet interested in hardware and IoT. We see #badgelife, #badgelove, SAOs and badge hacking as a great potential for securing IoT and keeping the power in the hands of the consumer by spreading knowledge about the craft/trade. Those involved should be celebrated for sharing their knowledge. Many of them do not like the limelight, so this gives us a chance to personally say thank you in a chill environment.
